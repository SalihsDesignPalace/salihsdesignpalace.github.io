About

Welcome to Salih's Design Palace.

I come from a neurodivergent family, and creativity has become an important part of how we navigate life together. Designing, creating, and handcrafting unique pieces allows me to focus, unwind, and find genuine joy in the creative process. What began as a personal passion gradually grew into a small family project. While I personally design and handcraft each creation, my family plays an important supporting role behind the scenes, helping to bring Salih's Design Palace to life.

Through this journey, I discovered that creativity is far more than a hobby. It provides challenge, purpose, and a positive outlet that supports my mental well-being. My family shares this experience, which is why everything we do is approached with care, patience, and attention to detail.

At Salih's Design Palace, you will find a collection of individually handcrafted decorative pieces, ornaments, and unique resin figures that I personally design, sculpt, and finish by hand. I work with epoxy resin and crete resin, carefully sculpting, detailing, and hand-painting every creation. Because each item is handmade from start to finish, no two pieces are ever exactly alike. Every creation is truly one of a kind.

Alongside my handmade creations, I am also developing a collection of Sensory Bags. These contain carefully selected sensory tools, comfort items, and practical aids that my family and I have personally found helpful in managing feelings of overwhelm, overstimulation, stress, and sensory challenges often experienced within the neurodivergent community.

For us, creativity is not only about making beautiful objects. The process of creating has become an important part of maintaining balance, focus, and well-being. We have also experienced the value of sensory supports in everyday life, which inspired us to share these carefully chosen resources with others.

My hope is that you will discover something here that brings comfort, enjoyment, inspiration, or support—whether for yourself, a family member, a friend, or someone you care about. Whether it is a unique handmade creation or a thoughtfully assembled sensory bag, every item is prepared with genuine care and attention.

Thank you for supporting my work and my creative journey. Your support means a great deal to me. Whether you choose to purchase one of my creations or simply share my website with others who may appreciate what I do, you are helping a small family project continue to grow.

You can find additional information about the materials I use, as well as details regarding privacy, terms and conditions, and product information elsewhere on the website.

Please take your time, have a look around, and enjoy exploring the collection. If you cannot find exactly what you are looking for, feel free to get in touch. Whenever possible, I will be happy to discuss custom creations and special requests.

Thank you for visiting Salih's Design Palace.
