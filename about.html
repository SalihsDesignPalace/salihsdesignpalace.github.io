About

Welcome to Salih's Design Palace.

Creativity has played an important role in our family life for many years. Within our family, several of us experience and interact with the world in different ways, giving us a personal understanding of sensory differences, individual support needs, and the importance of finding positive ways to build confidence, focus, wellbeing, and self-expression.

For me, designing, creating, and handcrafting unique pieces became far more than simply a hobby. It became a source of purpose, enjoyment, personal growth, and achievement. Through the creative process, I discovered a meaningful way to express myself, develop new skills, and turn ideas and imagination into something tangible and unique.

What began as a personal passion gradually grew into a small family project. While I personally design and handcraft each creation, my family plays an important supporting role behind the scenes, helping to bring Salih's Design Palace to life.

Through this journey, I came to appreciate how creativity can support wellbeing, encourage self-expression, build confidence, and provide a meaningful sense of achievement. These experiences have shaped the values behind everything we do, which is why every creation is approached with patience, care, and attention to detail.

At Salih's Design Palace, you will find a collection of individually handcrafted decorative pieces, ornaments, and unique resin figures that I personally design, sculpt, and finish by hand. I work with epoxy resin and crete resin, carefully sculpting, detailing, and hand-painting every creation. Because each item is handmade from start to finish, no two pieces are ever exactly alike. Every creation is truly one of a kind.

Alongside my handmade creations, I am also developing collections of Sensory Bags and Craft Sets.

The Sensory Bags contain carefully selected sensory tools, comfort items, and practical aids inspired by personal and family experience. These items have been chosen with an understanding of the importance of comfort, focus, regulation, confidence, and everyday wellbeing.

Our Craft Sets are designed to encourage creativity, imagination, learning, and enjoyable hands-on activities for children, families, and anyone who enjoys making something unique. They reflect the same belief that creativity can be both enjoyable and rewarding while helping people explore their own ideas and abilities.

For us, creativity is not only about making beautiful objects. The process of creating has become an important part of maintaining balance, focus, confidence, and wellbeing. It has shown us the value of creative expression, meaningful activities, and practical supports that can make everyday life more enjoyable and fulfilling.

My hope is that you will discover something here that brings comfort, enjoyment, inspiration, or support—whether for yourself, a family member, a friend, or someone you care about. Whether it is a unique handmade creation, a thoughtfully assembled sensory bag, or a creative craft set, every item is prepared with genuine care and attention.

Thank you for supporting my work and my creative journey. Your support means a great deal to me. Whether you choose to purchase one of my creations or simply share my website with others who may appreciate what I do, you are helping a small family project continue to grow.

You can find additional information about the materials I use, as well as details regarding privacy, terms and conditions, and product information elsewhere on the website.

Please take your time, have a look around, and enjoy exploring the collection. If you cannot find exactly what you are looking for, feel free to get in touch. Whenever possible, I will be happy to discuss custom creations and special requests.

Thank you for visiting Salih's Design Palace.
